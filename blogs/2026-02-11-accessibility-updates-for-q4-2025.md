For the last few months, the CMS team has been focused on building out the design system for Craft 6 . Every single component is being designed with accessibility in mind, and we’ll be able to address some of the trickier issues that we couldn’t address in a 5.x release. In addition, we closed the year with many accessibility improvements to this website and our Documentation site .
