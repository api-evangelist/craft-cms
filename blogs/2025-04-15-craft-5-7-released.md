Craft 5.7 is here, and it’s packed with tons of improvements that make working with nested content smoother and more intuitive than ever. Copy and paste elements. Entries and addresses now have “Copy” actions, which opens a special notification that keeps track of the copied elements, as you navigate to a section or field where the elements should be pasted.
