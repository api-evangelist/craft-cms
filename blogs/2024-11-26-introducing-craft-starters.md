We just launched a Starters page on the site, featuring a new “Basic Blog” starter, plus the WordPress-inspired blog starter announced last week . The new starter was designed to help developers familiarize themselves with Craft’s content modeling features quickly. It features: A “Home” single section A “Blog Posts” channel section A “Pages” structure section A “Guestbook Posts” channel section, demonstrating front-end submissions The project is available in two different front ends: Twig and Nuxt (headless), with more on the way!
