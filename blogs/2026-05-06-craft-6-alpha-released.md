Our Craft 6 journey just hit a major milestone: the codebase is now completely ported to Laravel. To mark the occasion, we just tagged version 6.0.0-alpha.1 , marking the beginning of the Craft 6 Alpha. This concludes a massive, year-long effort, and now we’re excited to move onto feature development.
