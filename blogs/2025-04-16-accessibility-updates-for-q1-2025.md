Now that Q1 has wrapped up and Craft 5.7 is out, here’s a look at the accessibility improvements we’ve made since our last accessibility update . General In Craft 5.6.7, a bug was fixed that led to inconsistent slideout behavior for users who preferred reduced motion — such as focus not being moved into slideouts, or being unable to activate certain slideouts via keyboard. We also resolved a bug in 5.6.11 where focus wasn’t returned to the action menu trigger after closing a field layout element’s settings slideout.
